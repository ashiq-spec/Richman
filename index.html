Our Story

Crafted With Precision.
Worn With Pride.

Richman Suits Gold was born from a singular obsession — the perfect suit. What started as a small atelier in Chennai has grown into the city's most trusted name in men's luxury tailoring, having dressed over 7,500 men across weddings, boardrooms, and milestone moments.

Every stitch we place carries a decade of expertise. We don't just tailor garments — we architect confidence. From the initial consultation to the final fitting, our master craftsmen treat each suit as a personal commission, not a transaction.
